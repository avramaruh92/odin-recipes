Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey melted cheese. It's a comforting meal that's perfect for family gatherings, holidays, or any time you crave something hearty and satisfying.

This recipe features a savory ground beef filling, a flavorful tomato sauce, and a blend of cheeses that melt together beautifully. Baked until golden and bubbly, lasagna is a crowd-pleaser that tastes even better the next day.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic and cook until fragrant. Add ground beef, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish. Layer noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and meat sauce. Repeat layers, finishing with meat sauce and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes, until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
